Meet the Artist

Hi, I’m Maphuela Sekgala, the artist behind Maps Arts. I’ve been passionate about art since I was five, exploring creativity with every pencil and brush I could find. My journey truly took off in 2017 when a friend showed me how to draw my own portrait—an experience that completely inspired me to pursue art seriously. Since then, I’ve honed my skills in drawing, painting, and design, creating pieces that tell a story and connect with people. I’ve faced betrayal—even from an art teacher and a best friend—but instead of breaking me, it built me. Art became my way to turn pain into strength and stories people can connect with. Through Maps Arts, I create not just pieces, but conversations. What story would you want art to tell for you?
